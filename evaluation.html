Appendix A

Guideline Status Comments
1.1 Complied Our only non-content media are images.

1.3 Complied The info is divided into clear section tags. There is no really important sequence. The main operation provided to users is the form to enter products. Each field in this form has a placeholder text.

All links that go to an external source have either text before them of what they are (email and tel) or have an alt to the linking image. Each section has a header.
