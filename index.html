About

Max Market is focussed on reducing the amount of steps your food product has to traverse, by ordering as locally as possible and delivering straight from our distribution centers. This way we can keep our products fresh, while also keeping transport costs low safing your money and also reducing exhaust fumes.

Our 5 key values

  1. Keeping the prices as low as possible
  2. Being environmentally conscious
  3. Making our costumers feel welcomed and valued
  4. Quick delivery
  5. Efficiency
